Slither.io

This game is you being a snake, and eating other snakes. If an other snake`s head touches your body, they turn into "particles", and you can eat them.If you hold down right click, you get a boost.

  • Other people CAN eat the "particles" of a snake you just killed.
  • If you use the boost, you will loose mass, leaving a trail of "particles" behind.
  • if touch another snake`s body, you will turn into "particles" and return to the start screen.
